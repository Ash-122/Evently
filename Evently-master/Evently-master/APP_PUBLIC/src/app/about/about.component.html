What Is Evently?


Evently is an app for scheduling appointments, meetings, and events. Its goal is to eliminate the problematic back-and-forth when trying to nail down times. Rather than email chains and phone tag, you can send your availability with a Evently link (even if the people booking time with you don't use Evently). Invitees will see all the available openings in your schedule and can select the one that suits them best. That ensures the ideal time slot for both parties, eliminates time zone confusion, and avoids the strings of conversations about who's available when.

Overview

Schedule meetings without the hassle. Never get double booked. Evently works with your calendar to automate appointment scheduling. Sign up for your free Evently account, then use the Evently for Chrome extension to: - Access all of your event types right from your Chrome browser - Select and share your available times to meet in just a few clicks - Mark and save event types as your favorites for even quicker access - Choose specific time slots to display in emails to quickly set up one-off meetings - Add notes to your event types that only you and your team can see With our Chrome extension, you can also access Evently directly from Gmail to: - Quickly set up one-off meetings - Share the links to your Evently event scheduling pages

About Evently

Evently connects with your personal and business calendars to save you time and simplify the tasks involved in meeting with people outside your company. Now you can connect and agree with your invitees on the best time to meet and schedule your meetings without the back-and-forth emails.
